Course Description

Our comprehensive Generative AI course at Digital Nexus AI. Tailored for both beginners and experienced professionals, this course delves into the fascinating world of generative models, empowering you with the knowledge and skills to innovate and excel in AI-driven industries. By completing this course, you'll gain a deep understanding of Generative AI and the ability to develop advanced generative models. Whether you aim to advance your career, innovate in your current role, or explore new creative avenues, our Generative AI course equips you with the tools and insights to succeed.

What you'll learn

1. You will learn how to generate new content such as images, text, and even source code among others.
2. You will learn about LLMs, ChatGPT, Prompt Engineering, and Vector databases.
3. By learning Gen AI you are gaining an understanding of cutting-edge technology and a lot of new concepts.
4. You will learn how to write Gen AI programs to build a custom GPT like chatPDF and others.
