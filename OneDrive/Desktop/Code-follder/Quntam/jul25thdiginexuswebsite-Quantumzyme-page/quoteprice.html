Quote Your Price

With ‘Quote Your Price’, you can offer the instructor a price you’re willing to pay for the course. The instructor can accept or decline your quote.

‘Quote Your Price’ is a great way to buy the course you’ve been look for at a bargain price. As well as saving money, you won’t have to wait for the listing to end to buy your course, if the instructor accept your offer

How to 'Quote Your Price'

You can make ‘Quote Your Price’ on the Course page that have a ‘Quote Price’ button .

Once you’ve found an course you want to buy the accepts ‘Quote Your Price’, here’s how to make your quote:

  1. Choose ‘Quote Price’ .
  2. Enter your quote. If you like, you can add a message to the instructor.
  3. Select Continue

If the instructor accept or decline your quote you will be notified via e-maill and receive a notification on your personal Dashboard.
